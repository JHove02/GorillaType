About Us
GorillaType was created with one goal in mind: to serve those looking for a fun and relaxing typing test (and to complete a group project we were working on). Carson Bring, Ben Rybacki, Isaac Landin, and Jordan Hove spent hours developing this website to obtain the A as well as create a typing test that they were proud of. Throughout the development of this project, we learned new things, challenged ourselves, and ultimately produced work that we were proud of. Our website was inspired by a typing website we enjoiy called MonkeyType. This website is purely for educational use as an assignment for NDSU CSCI 313. We hope you enjoy our site!
